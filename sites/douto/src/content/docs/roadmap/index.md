# Roadmap

Douto's roadmap now starts from a simple definition:

**Douto is Jude.md's internal doctrine supplier to Valter.**

## Consolidated Direction

- primary consumer: Valter;
- initial authority: contract law and civil procedure;
- retrieval and synthesis remain in scope;
- quality comes before expansion;
- markdown/Obsidian remains an editorial layer, not the product center.

## Current State

- there is a local pipeline;
- there is local CLI search;
- there is no mature programmatic delivery yet;
- there is no trustworthy release gate for synthesis.

## Official Sequence

1. Reproducible foundation
2. Quality gate
3. Delivery contract to Valter
4. Explainable retrieval
5. Synthesis with its own gate

## Canonical Backlog

Douto's canonical backlog lives in Linear under `repo/douto`, from `SEN-447` through `SEN-468`, with `blockedBy` enforcing execution order.

## Work Intentionally Deferred

- hierarchical ranking and similar explorations wait until after the baseline (`SEN-446`)
- direct real-time or MCP-facing surfaces stay behind good delivery to Valter
