# FAQ

Language: [pt-BR](pt-br/faq.md) | `English`

## Is Silo already publicly available?

Not yet. The product is still in a private build and validation phase.

## Does Silo replace Claude or other LLMs?

No. The product thesis is to amplify LLMs with structured legal data,
grounding, and traceability.

## Is Silo open source?

No. This repository is public for informational purposes, but the product is
not open source. The internal engines, main codebase, and operational assets
remain private and proprietary.

## Is the focus on case law or legislation?

Both are part of the proposal, alongside document context and traceable
explanation. The goal is to avoid a fragmented legal workflow.

## Does this repository contain the main codebase?

No. The main product code remains in private, specialized repositories.
This repository is focused on public-facing documentation.

## Who is the target audience?

The initial focus is on Brazilian lawyers who need legal research, case
context, and diagnostic support with more reliability than a purely generic
workflow.
