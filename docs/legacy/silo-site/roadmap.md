# Public Roadmap

Language: [pt-BR](pt-br/roadmap.md) | `English`

The roadmap below summarizes the current direction of the product at a high
level.

It exists to communicate intent and priorities, not to expose the internal
backlog line by line.

## 1. Foundation

Goal: ensure the system has the data base, signals, and structure required to
produce reliable legal intelligence.

Focus:

- organize and prioritize corpora
- deepen the legal base in the right niches
- stabilize contracts between internal layers

## 2. The 360 moment

Goal: demonstrate that Silo produces a legal reading that is better than a
workflow based only on a general-purpose LLM.

Focus:

- comparative tests
- clear and inspectable deltas
- real cases with explainable gains

## 3. External validation

Goal: confirm value with lawyers outside the core building team.

Focus:

- use in real cases
- qualitative feedback
- evidence of practical utility

## 4. Flywheel

Goal: prove that more data and more usage improve the system itself.

Focus:

- learn from corpus activity
- improve prioritization
- raise diagnostic quality over time

## 5. Product surface

Goal: consolidate the main user experience on top of the validated core.

Focus:

- onboarding clarity
- central product experience
- launch and demonstration narrative

## Note

Stages, names, and deliverables may evolve. This document is a public summary
of the product direction, not a detailed delivery commitment.
